# Code of Conduct

Reddix should be a focused, professional project space.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Critique code, designs, and decisions rather than people.
- Assume public comments and issues may be read by new contributors.
- Keep security-sensitive details out of public issues.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory
language, doxxing, threats, or publishing another person's private information.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when
behavior harms the project or contributors.
